Research agenda

Making modern AI more private, robust, and efficient.

Designing compressed and differentially private fine-tuning methods that improve privacy-utility tradeoffs under strict privacy budgets.

TT-LoRA & efficient adaptation

Exploring Tensor-Train decomposition for parameter-efficient fine-tuning, regularization, and low-footprint adaptation of large models.

Security & adversarial robustness

Studying membership inference, adversarial malware analysis, and trustworthy AI evaluation for high-consequence systems.
